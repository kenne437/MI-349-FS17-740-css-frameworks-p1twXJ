Anna's Library Clean Out!

My team and I work hard to clean out your library. We are interested in collecting books such as the ones at the top of this page. We charge $10 per hour and could usually take 5-6 hours because we normally end up reading the first few chapters to decide if we need to take them off your hands.

DESCRIPTION:
We are a team of eleven book lovers. We restore old books that people don't use anymore or simply don't like. Preferably crime or mystery novels are what we are interested in but we also would like to exapand our horizons by trying different genres.
PRICES:
Our hourly rate is $10 per hour but depending on the size of your library we will need a specific number of people. Each person gets paid $10 per hour, so if you need 2 people it will be $20 per hour and so on.

EMPLOYEES:
Anna, Brad, Elisabeth, Lauren, Rachel, Trent, Luke,
Jack, Rachel, Patrick, and Jourdan

ABOUT:
We believe that everybody should be reading even if its for different reasons. Some love reading to gain knowledge and others read just for enjoyment. However, many books are on shelves and dont end up moving and that's where we come in. We collect books that are unused and we give them to others to gain knowledge or pure enjoyment. Putting smiles on faces since 1989.
